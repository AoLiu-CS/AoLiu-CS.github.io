Short Bio

Ao Liu received his Ph.D. in Computer Science and M.Eng. in Material Engineering from Rensselaer Polytechnic Institute (RPI). Prior to that, he received his B.S. in Mathematics and Physics, and a minor degree in Computer Technology from Tsinghua University. He has broad interests in the theoretical aspects of Machine Learning (recommendation systems, learning to rank, robustness, and etc.), Differential Privacy, and Computational Social Choice. Ao Liu is also a recipient of RPI-IBM AI Horizon scholarship and RPI Presidential Graduate Research Fellowship.
